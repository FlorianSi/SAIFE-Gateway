## 1. Information on Data Processing
We use the SAIFE (Safe AI in Formal Education) platform to provide an AI-supported learning environment.

## 2. Processed Data
- **Chat History:** Stored for a maximum of 7 days.
- **Safety Logs (Penalties/Rejections):** Stored for a maximum of 24 hours.
- **Crisis Records:** Retained for 12 months to comply with child protection mandates.
- **Telemetry Data:** Numeric counts and category-codes only (30 days max).

## 3. Purpose and Legal Basis
Processing is conducted under Art. 6(1)(e) General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (Public interest) and Art. 9(2)(g) GDPR (Substantial public interest for safety records).

## 4. Data Subject Rights
You have the right to access (Art. 15), rectify (Art. 16), or erase (Art. 17) your data.
*Note: Erasure requests for crisis events require explicit exemption flags due to school safety mandates.*
