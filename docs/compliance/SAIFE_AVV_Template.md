This Data Processing Agreement (Auftragsverarbeitungsvertrag) template is provided for integrators of the SAIFE system pursuant to Art. 28 General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 1. Subject Matter of the Processing
The processor processes personal data on behalf of the controller to provide the services agreed upon in the main contract (provision of the AI-supported pedagogical chatbot).

## 2. Nature and Purpose of the Processing
- Chat histories and metadata (max. 7 days)
- Telemetry data (anonymized/pseudonymized, max. 30 days)
- Crisis escalation logs (max. 12 months)

## 3. Type of Data
Personal data of students (users) and teachers (moderators), including special categories (health data in crisis cases pursuant to Art. 9 GDPR).

## 4. Authority to Issue Instructions
The processor processes data only on documented instructions from the controller.
